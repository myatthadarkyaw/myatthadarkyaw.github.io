About Us

We are a female-owned Australian brand, located in south-west Sydney. Beads to Dream means that our jewelry will definitely make your dream jewelry come true.

At Beads to Dream, our goal is to empower women through embracing individuality. While our brand is inspired by a mix of 90s fashion and luxe street style, we want our accessories to accentuate your personal style. Whether you're a minimalist or you're extra AF, we want you our accessories to make you feel confident!

As a small business, we want to take advantage of this time to build relationships with our customers. We hope that when you purchase from Beads to Dream, it's an experience not just a transaction.

We look forward to receiving your feedback so that we are able to make your shopping experience with Beads to Dream as enjoyable as possible!

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to Contact Us!
